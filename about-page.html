Our Mission

Creating opportunities and encouraging health care delivery access, through social and educational development for neglected/vulnerable pregnant women, women with disability, the girl child, orphans and vulnerable children and new born.

Our Vision

A society where every vulnerable woman/girl child and children are healthy, wealthy and informed.

Our Objectives

  1. To provide medical assistance for vulnerable/neglected pregnant women, women with disability, the girl child, orphans and vulnerable children.
  2. To assist in the provision of nursing materials, including infant feeding options where breast feeding is not possible.
  3. To work for the social development of under privileged households through women empowerment and education.
  4. To strengthen health systems by facilitating trainings and continuous education in health qualities with particular focus on maternal, new born and child health.
  5. To create awareness among women and girls on human rights and sexual and gender-based violence (SGBV).
